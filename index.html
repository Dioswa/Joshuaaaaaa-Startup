Our Services

Swedish Massage

Experience the timeless art of relaxation with our Swedish Massage. Designed to ease tension and rejuvenate the body, this gentle yet effective massage uses long, flowing strokes to improve circulation and melt away stress, leaving you refreshed and revitalized.

Hot Stone Massage

Let the warmth of smooth, heated stones transport you to a realm of tranquility. Our Hot Stone Massage combines therapeutic heat with gentle pressure to release deep-seated tension, soothe sore muscles, and promote a profound sense of calm and well-being.

Aromatherapy Massage

Indulge your senses with our Aromatherapy Massage, a harmonious blend of soothing touch and essential oils. Each oil is carefully selected to enhance relaxation, uplift the spirit, and restore balance, leaving you enveloped in serenity and a sense of renewal

Swedish Massage
A soothing massage technique using heated stones to deeply relax muscles and improve circulation.

Benefits
  • Eases muscle tension and stiffness
  • Promotes deeper relaxation
  • Boosts blood flow and detoxification
What to Expect
  • Warm stones placed on key areas of the body
  • Gentle pressure and smooth strokes with heated stones
Before Your Session
  • Arrive well-hydrated and wear loose clothing
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol before your session
Aftercare Tips
  • Drink plenty of water to flush out toxins
  • Rest and avoid intense physical activity for a day
Duration: 30-50 mins
